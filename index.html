Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 5, 2025

Welcome to our Resume Builder App. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

Our app allows you to create and manage resumes. All personal data (such as name, contact details, education, and work history) entered in the app is stored only locally on your device. We do not upload, share, or store this data on any server controlled by us.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party SDKs and services for improving app performance and providing ads:

  • Google Firebase – for analytics, crash reporting, and improving app stability

These third-party services may collect certain information automatically, such as device type, app usage, and identifiers. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

How We Use Information

We do not access or collect your resume data. Information gathered by third-party SDKs is used only to improve user experience, provide relevant ads, and maintain app functionality.

Data Security

Since your resume data is stored only locally, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device. We recommend using device-level security features such as PIN, password, or biometric authentication.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
