Privacy Policy for CV Preparation: Resume Builder

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which the CV Preparation: Resume Builder mobile application ("App") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the App. This privacy policy applies to the App and all products and services offered by Faisal ur Rehman.

1. Personal Data and Local Storage

CV Preparation: Resume Builder does not collect any personal data from its users. All information that users input into the App, such as names, contact details, work experience, education, skills, and other resume-related content, is **stored exclusively on the user's device** and is not transmitted to or stored on any server controlled by Faisal ur Rehman.

The App does not require users to register an account or provide any personal information to use its core features. Your resume data is yours and remains private to your device.

2. Third-Party Services and Data Processing

While the App itself does not collect personal data, it does utilize third-party services that may collect anonymized, non-personal data for purposes such as analytics, crash reporting, and the operation of AI features and advertising. These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.

2.1. AI Services (Google Gemini API)

The App uses the **Google Gemini API** to power intelligent features, including generating career objectives, skill summaries, and personalized interview questions based on your CV content.

When you use the 'Generate with AI' feature, the relevant, user-provided text content (e.g., job title, project descriptions) from your locally stored CV is **temporarily transmitted** to the Gemini API for processing. The generated AI response is then returned to your device.

Data Handling: We only transmit the minimum necessary text content required to generate the AI response. We **do not store** this transmitted content on any server controlled by us, and we adhere strictly to the Gemini API usage policies regarding user privacy.

2.2. Google AdMob

The App uses Google AdMob, an advertising service provided by Google Ireland Limited. AdMob may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect data for personalized advertising. This data is typically non-personal and includes information such as device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data. This is done to provide relevant advertisements.

For more information on how AdMob collects and processes data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.3. Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)

The App uses Google Firebase, a development platform provided by Google. Specifically, Firebase is used for:

  • Firebase Analytics: To understand how users engage with the App, such as app usage patterns, session duration, and feature engagement. This data is anonymized and helps improve the App's performance and user experience.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To collect crash reports, which helps in identifying and fixing bugs and improving the App's stability. Crash reports contain technical information about the crash but do not include personal user data.

For more information on how Firebase collects and processes data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.4. In-App Purchases and Financial Data (Apple)

If you choose to purchase an auto-renewing subscription or any other product within the App, the transaction will be processed by Apple through the **Apple App Store**. We **do not collect or store your financial information**, such as credit card numbers or banking details.

We receive non-personal transaction confirmation data from Apple, such as the purchase date, the subscription status (active/inactive), and a unique transaction identifier, which we use solely for **verifying your access to premium features** and managing the subscription within the App. All payment details are managed securely by Apple, and their processing is governed by the Apple Media Services Terms and Conditions and their Privacy Policy.

3. Data Security and Sharing

As stated in Section 1, all resume content and personal details you enter into the **CV Preparation: Resume Builder** app are stored locally on your device. Faisal ur Rehman does not have access to this data. The security of your data depends on the security of your device and any backup solutions you use (e.g., iCloud backups).

When you choose to export or share your resume (e.g., as a PDF), the App utilizes standard device sharing functionalities. Any data shared or exported through these channels will be subject to the privacy policies of the respective third-party services (e.g., email providers, cloud storage services).
